
     Give the Cookie Name that you are looking for on the command line.
     The program will give you the Binary and Hex values of that Cookie,
     if found, or will tell you if it can't find it.

     Remember that Cookies are Case Sensitive! (i.e. don't look for _snd)

     e.g.     _SND will return 1f on the falcon.
