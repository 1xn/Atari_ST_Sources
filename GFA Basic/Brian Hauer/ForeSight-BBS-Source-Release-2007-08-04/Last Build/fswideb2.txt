Foresight - The premier donation-ware BBS program for your
Atari ST or STE from TSO Software.  Features galore:
messages, e-mail, f-mail, QWK/REP, full ANSI, UBT, and VT52,
great doors support, voting booths, FSN networking of
messages and e-mail, uni- and bi-directional protocol
support, much more. Requires 2+ megs, color monitor.
