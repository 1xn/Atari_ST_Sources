18.26 Viking


Die Viking Grafikkarte für Mega ST


The viking card is made by Atari for the SM194 (ECL 19' screen) with DB9 connector. It plugs in the 68K MegaBus and uses a driver sm194drv in 1280*960*monochrome, NVDI can be used with it (NVDI4, version 5 not tested).

The empty socket is for 68881 FPU. (!Nl)
