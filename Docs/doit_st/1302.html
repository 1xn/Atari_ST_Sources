19.2 Tweety Board


Tweety Board



In an 1040STf a ribbon cable and header has been soldered ontop of IC U19 and the board at the other end of this cable reads:
"Practical Solutions Tweety Board Ver 1.4 1988"

The tweety board is a rather useless a poor quality sound enhancement.

It adds pseudo-stereo to the YM soundchip output. The STE's DMA sound is far better. I don't know of any software that took advantage of the tweety's other features.
